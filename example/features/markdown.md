## Math Equations

You can use TeX equations for mathematical formulas.

### Inline Math

This is an inline equation: $E = mc^2$.

### Display Math

This is a display equation:

$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

Another example with a matrix:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$
